Welcome to Beulah House of Prayer

Welcome to Beulah House of Prayer, located at Kunu Road, Townend Drive, Auki, Malaita Province. We are a multidenominational fellowship with a heartfelt desire to share the Good News with the Beulah community and surrounding areas within Auki Township.

About Us

Beulah Gateway Prayer Centre is a multidenominational fellowship with a heartfelt desire to share the Good News with the Beulah community and surrounding areas within Auki Township. The church and its ministries began as a family fellowship led by Elder Cornelius Ngui, his sons, and their families. In 2017, the Lord mandated the family to erect a small church building at Kunu, which has since grown into the thriving ministry it is today.

Located in Kunu, close to the famous Osi Lake. We are affiliated with the Pacific Prayer Movement in Malaita, sharing a vision of forming a united Body of Christ eager to serve God in every possible way.
